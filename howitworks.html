How It Works

Businesses

  • Donate your good uneaten food and volunteers pick up and deliver it to those in need.
  • Reduce your businesses food waste and make a donation at the same time.
  • Track your Replate requests and see when they have been completed.

Individuals

  • Volunteers sign up and transport the food to places in need.
  • View the Replate requests in your area.
  • Accept your and complete your the delivery of requests.
